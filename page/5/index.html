How to remove keyring prompt in Chromium

How to remove * keyring prompt* in Chromium.
I am using ElementaryOS. After I changed my passwd, this issue disturbs me a lot.
Now I find a method to solve it.

Before it, I must warn you this will need you reconfigure some apps, such as fcitx.

  • Open files and set show hidden files
  • Try to delete file directions: .cache , .config, .local/share/
    #If system tell you it can’t be deleted, choose skip
  • Log out and log in again

Done! Open Chromium and check.
